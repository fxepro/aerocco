The Aerocco Advantage

Our core technologies work in synergy to create a hyper-efficient, sustainable, and scalable farming ecosystem.

Vertical Aeroponic Pods

Our modular pods provide a nutrient-rich mist directly to plant roots, maximizing growth and minimizing water usage in a space-efficient vertical layout.

Automated Conveyor System

A centralized conveyor system automates the movement of pods from planting to harvesting, drastically reducing labor and improving operational flow.

Integrated Solar Covers

The terrain-adaptive structure is covered by solar panels, generating clean energy to power the farm's systems and contributing to its sustainability.
